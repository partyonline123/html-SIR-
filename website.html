About

At the School of Informatics and Robotics, we are passionate about empowering the next generation of tech innovators through our Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science. Our curriculum is thoughtfully designed to cover key areas such as algorithms, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, data science and more ensuring that students gain both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. We believe in the power of hands-on learning. Our state-of-the-art laboratories and advanced robotics equipment provide students with the opportunity to engage in collaborative projects that connect technology to real-world challenges. Here, you’ll not only learn how to code but also how to think critically and creatively to solve complex problems. Our dedicated faculty—comprised of industry professionals and leading researchers—are committed to guiding you on your journey. Join us at the School of Informatics and Robotics, and let’s shape the future of technology together!
